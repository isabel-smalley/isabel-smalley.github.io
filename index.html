About Me

I am an undergraduate student pursuing a double major in Biology and Computer Science, with a deep-rooted passion for plant science and evolutionary biology. My academic journey has been complemented by 3 and a half years of research experience in plant science, where I have developed a keen interest in the genomic and evolutionary aspects of plant science. I am now seeking PhD graduate school programs that align with my interests in plant science and evolutionary biology, with a particular focus on genomics and evolution. My goal is to contribute to cutting-edge research projects that explore the genetic and evolutionary mechanisms underlying speciation and hybridization, with a special interest in non-diploid plant systems.
